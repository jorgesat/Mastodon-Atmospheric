⛈️ Atmospheric and air quality scientists

Follow atmospheric and air quality scientists on Mastodon

You want to get in touch with atmospheric and AQ scientists from around the world on Mastodon? This page lists accounts from atmospheric and AQ scientists and institutions and offers an easy method to follow multiple accounts at once. Just decide if you want to follow all or only some of the accounts listed below. You are provided with a .csv-file, which you can upload in Mastodon in order to follow all accounts included. Here you can find a short instruction, on how to do this.

If you'd like to be added to the list please fill in this form or add yourself directly by editing the list on Github. If you want to edit some of the information given i.e., misspellings, errors, or get removed from the list message me at @jorgeluis@pub.solar.

Note: This is an opt-in list created by @rachelOB1@mastodon.social: accounts are only added by explicit consent. It is a starting point for connecting with the atmospheric community in the Fediverse, but is by no means a complete register. Hashtags matter in Mastodon, so use and follow hashtags such as #AtmosSci #AtmosChem #AirPollution #AirQuality to find and be found!
